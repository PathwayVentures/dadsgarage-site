Our Services
Spark Plug Job

Includes plugs, boots, dielectric grease.
Pro Tip: Replace boots and use dielectric grease for longer life.
Brake Job (Front/Rear)

Includes pads, rotors, hardware, brake cleaner, caliper grease.
Pro Tip: Clean all hardware, always lube caliper pins, use fresh brake cleaner.
Radiator Flush

Includes flush, coolant, check hoses & clamps.
Pro Tip: Inspect all hoses, pressure test after refill.
Oil Change

Includes oil, filter, crush washer.
Pro Tip: Replace crush washer every time, check for leaks.
Battery Replacement

Includes battery, terminal cleaning.
Pro Tip: Coat terminals with anti-corrosion spray.
Alternator/Starter

Includes part, labor, diagnostic.
Pro Tip: Always check belt tension and grounds.
